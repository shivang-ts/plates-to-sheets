Privacy Policy — Plates to Sheets

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Plates to Sheets is a Chrome extension that helps restaurant partners export order history from Swiggy Partner and Zomato Partner to CSV files on your device.

What we collect

The extension stores the minimum data needed to call partner APIs on your behalf:

  • Swiggy: access token and restaurant ID (on your device only).
  • Zomato: restaurant ID, CSRF tokens, and session auth token (browser session storage).
  • Preferences: selected platform and optional debug setting.

We do not store full browser cookie strings. Session cookies are read from Chrome at export time and are not uploaded elsewhere.

During export, order and customer fields (such as names, areas, and order details) are processed locally on your device to build the CSV file. This data is not sent to servers operated by the extension author.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No data sent to servers operated by the extension author.
  • No sale or sharing of your order data with third parties.

Where data lives

  • Credentials and settings: chrome.storage.local on your computer.
  • Zomato session tokens: chrome.storage.session (cleared when the browser session ends).
  • Exported CSV files: saved via Chrome Downloads to a location you choose.

Third-party services

The extension communicates directly with Swiggy and Zomato partner APIs using your logged-in browser session. Their privacy policies apply to data held on their platforms.

Permissions

  • storage — save credentials and preferences locally.
  • cookies — read Zomato session cookies at export time.
  • webRequest — capture auth headers when you use the partner dashboard.
  • tabs — open partner login and help pages.
  • downloads — save CSV exports.

Data retention & deletion

Click Clear credentials in the extension popup to remove stored tokens. Uninstalling the extension removes extension storage. CSV files you downloaded remain on your disk.
