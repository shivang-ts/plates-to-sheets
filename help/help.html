Partner Orders Exporter — Help

Export order history from Swiggy Partner or Zomato Partner to CSV on your computer.

Getting started

  1. Choose Swiggy or Zomato in the extension popup.
  2. Click Open Partner Dashboard and log in if needed.
  3. Stay on the dashboard for a few seconds so your session is captured.
  4. Re-open the popup — status should show Logged in.
  5. Click Fetch & Export CSV.

During export

  • A progress bar shows the current step (loading history, order details, customer info).
  • Click Cancel export to stop — no file is saved if you cancel mid-way.
  • Large date ranges may take several minutes on Zomato (one API call per order).

Swiggy tips

  • Use the /food dashboard after login.
  • If capture fails, paste access_token and restaurantId under Manual credentials (from DevTools → Network on any rms.swiggy.com request).

Zomato tips

  • Select the correct outlet on the partner dashboard before exporting.
  • Restaurant ID should look like 22737520 (not a long timestamp).
  • Date range defaults to today only; change start/end dates to export a wider range.
  • After reloading the extension, refresh any open partner tabs.

Troubleshooting

  • Not logged in — Open the partner dashboard again and wait a few seconds.
  • Access denied / wrong outlet — Switch to the correct restaurant in the partner app.
  • No orders — Try a wider date range (Zomato) or confirm orders exist in the dashboard.
  • Extension context invalidated — Reload the extension, then refresh partner tabs.

Not affiliated with or endorsed by Swiggy or Zomato. Order data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to third-party servers.
